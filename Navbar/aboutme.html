About Me

For as long as I can remember, I have wanted to make music for a living, I like to think that's my dad's fault. when I was a kid, he would sing me a song every night before bed. Jim Croce, The Carpenters, and James Taylor. it was the best part of my day, during the best part of my life. I remember how those songs used to make me feel, even though my father said he hated his singing voice, he was my favorite musician.

I started producing my own music as a hobby in 2016 while I was still a high school student and, despite spending so much time on it, didn't see it as anything more than a fun hobby. I remember being stressed out about what I would do after I graduated, when i realized that the only thing that I enjoyed doing, making music, might have careers outside of being the artist. Everyone needs instrumentals, Promotion, merchandise, all the behind the scenes stuff. I realized that I could be that guy.

On top of over 5 years of experience in both graphic design and music production, I have also graduated from the Recording Arts & Sound Production program at F.I.R.S.T. Institute, and I am currently attending their Graphic Design and Web Development program.

I am on track to graduate in July 2022
