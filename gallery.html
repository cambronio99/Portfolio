Tabula Rasa

Tabula Rasa is an album I released in four parts during the Summer of 2024. It was a collection of Music that I had produced & recorded starting in 2018, and when my Hard Drive corrupted in Late 2023, I realized that in my pursuit of perfection, I had gotten caught in a loop of never actually releasing any music. Hence, Tabula Rasa, referring to the idea of a "Clean Slate."
